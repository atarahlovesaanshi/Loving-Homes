PREPARING FOR THE STAY

What to Pack for your Pup

We provide luxury bedding and bowls, but we recommend bringing a few items from home to help your dog settle in.

  • Favorite Toy: Something familiar for quiet time in the "Zen Zone."
  • Current Food: To avoid tummy upsets (unless you've selected our organic treat package).
  • Leash & Harness: For those 1-on-1 woodland walks.
  • Special Medication: We have a 24-hour on-call vet to assist with administration.
