Our Story

WHO WE ARE

A home away from home since 2017.

Founded in the heart of Hong Kong, Loving Homes began with a simple belief: every dog deserves a vacation as luxurious as their owner's. We aren't just a boarding facility; we are a dedicated retreat designed to reduce stress and promote happiness.

Our team consists of certified animal behaviorists and passionate caregivers who treat every guest like family. From our climate-controlled suites to our lush woodland walks, every detail is crafted for comfort.

Our Humble Beginnings

What started as a small family-run boarding house in 2017 has grown into Hong Kong's most awarded dog hotel. We realized that many pet owners felt guilty leaving their dogs in cramped, loud kennels. Loving Homes was built to be the opposite: a quiet, aromatic, and spacious sanctuary.

The "Luxury" Standard

To us, luxury isn't just about underfloor heating or TVs in rooms—it's about the quality of time. It's the 1-on-1 woodland walks, the specialized organic treats, and the 24/7 medical supervision that truly defines a premium stay.
